Write with more confidence across the web

You can enter a few words or a first draft to get AI writing suggestions from Chrome.

  1. In settings, turn on Help me write
  2. Go to a site where you want to write.
  3. Right-click on any open text box and select Help me write, then enter a few words or a first draft into the prompt box.
  4. Click Create to get a writing suggestion. If needed, you can change the length or tone of the suggestion.
  5. When you’re happy with the suggestion, click Insert to paste it into the open text box.

Create a unique image using AI to set as your Chrome theme

Combine your imagination with subject, style, mood, and color options to bring your custom theme to life.

  1. In settings, turn on Create themes with AI.
  2. Open a New tab and at the bottom of the page, click Customize Chrome
  3. Under Appearance, click Change theme.
  4. Under Themes, click Create with AI.
  5. Choose from subjects, styles, moods, and colors, and click Create.

Smartly organize your open tabs

To help you stay organized, Chrome can suggest and help you create tab groups.

  1. In settings, turn on Tab organizer.
  2. At the top left of your browser, click Tab search.
  3. Under the Organize tabs section, click Check now to get a tab group suggestion.
  4. You can edit the group name or remove tabs.
  5. When you’re happy with the group, click Create group.
